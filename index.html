Core Analytical Modules

In-silico Discovery (Topo-GNN)

Calculates Geometric Tropism Rankings and extracts "Novel Targets" using PyVis heterogeneous pathways to map external pharmacological modalities to anatomical nodes.

🌐

3D Manifold Mapping

Proves the utilization of Geodesic distances over classical Euclidean metrics by projecting physiological pathways onto a curved 3D WebGL space.

📐

TDA Filtration Simulator

Interactively tracks real-time changes in Betti numbers (β₀ for disconnected components, β₁ for topological cycles) across varying distance radiuses (ε).

📈

Real-Time Analytics

Tracks live topological biomarkers, simulating homeostatic regulation and network stabilization pre- and post-clinical intervention.
